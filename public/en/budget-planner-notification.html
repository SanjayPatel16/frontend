Changes to the Budget Planner
What's happening?
We've updated the detailed Budget Planner.
We'll be taking down the short Budget Planner on 15th January 2017.
What it means for you
If you use the short form planner
You need to save your details and transfer them to the detailed Budget planner.
The short Budget planner and any data you’ve stored on it will disappear on 15th January 2017, but...
You have 12 weeks to download your budget as a spreadsheet.
Use the new Budget Planner
If you use the long form planner
You don't have to do anything.
The detailed Budget Planner has been updated.
Any information you have saved will be available on the new version.
Use the new Budget Planner
Why are we doing this?
To give you a better and easier way to plan your budget. The new version is:
More accurate - Let’s you include more details about your money for a more precise breakdown.
Easier to use - Designed to use on both desktop and mobile devices.
Tailored to you - Personalised guidance based on your circumstances.
